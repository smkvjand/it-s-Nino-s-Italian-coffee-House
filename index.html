Our Story

A little corner
of Italy in Bachupally

Nino's Italian Coffee House was built on a simple love of good food, great coffee, and warm company. Located in the Bowrampet neighbourhood near Okridge School on Medchal Road, we've been a favourite family spot since day one.

Whether you come for a morning cappuccino, a weekday lunch with colleagues, or a relaxed family dinner, you'll always find a familiar face and a fresh meal at Nino's.
